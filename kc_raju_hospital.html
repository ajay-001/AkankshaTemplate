KC Raju Multispeciality Hospital


K C Raju is a multi-specialty hospital located Lingarajapuram, Bangalore and is dedicated for providing affordable and specialized treatment to the patients since 1976.

Generations of patients have ben availing quality healthcare at an affordable cost. Our vision is to continue to serve our patients and provide focused care to improve health outcomes. In keeping with this, our expert consultants and nursing staff are committed to patient care and cure.

Specialties

  • Family Medicine
  • OBG and Infertility
  • General Medicine
  • Pediatrics
  • General Surgery
  • Laparoscopic surgeries
  • Orthopedics
  • Cancer Surgeries
  • ENT
  • Psychiatry
  • Sexual Medicine
  • Urology
  • Andrology

Facilities

  • Multispeciality lab (Biochemistry, Pathology, Microbiology and radiology)
  • Ultrasound and doopler
  • Sonomammogram
  • X ray
  • ECG
  • 2D Echo cardiography
  • Physiotherapy
  • Infertility treatment (IUI/IVF)
  • ICU
  • Ambulance Services
