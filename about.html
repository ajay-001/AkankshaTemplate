About Aakanksha


  • Aakanksha Fertility Centre was established with an intention of providing the global standards of the treatment at an affordable cost

  • Our philosophy is: Your Dream is Ours

  • Aakanksha Fertility Center is a team of scientific, nursing and support staff to assist you with expert care

  • The majority of fertility cases (both male and female factors) are treated at Aakanksha Fertility Clinic, using advanced medical, surgical and scientific techniques

Specialist

  • Dr. Mangala Devi K R , MBBS, MS (OBG)
  • Consultant Obstetrician and Gynecologist
  • Certified Fertility Specialist (NUHS, Singapore)
  • Dr. Mangala Devi K R is a practicing Obstetrician, Gynecologist and fertility specialist, in clinical practice since many years
  • A scholar from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Dr. Mangala, commenced her specialization in Obstetrics & Gynecology from M S Ramaiah Medical College, Bangalore since 2007. She has been working as Consultant Gynecologist with the renowned K C Raju Multispecialty Hospital
  • A certified fertility specialist by NUHS Singapore, she has been associated with Morpheus IVF fertility center for the last three years providing infertility treatment in-line with international standards. She has performed many cycles of IVF / ICSI and ovulation induction successfully, with exceptional successes and delivering joy to couples
  • As a scientifically driven practitioner, Dr. Mangala has been involved in clinical research in Obstetrics and Gynecology. With her inclination towards social cause, Dr. Mangala provides awareness programs for adolescent girls, training for perinatal mothers and corporate professionals on the various aspects of women health.
  • Dr. Mangala continues her contribution towards academics by presenting scientific papers/research on myriad aspects of infertility at various international/national conferences and workshops regularly

Team

  • Aakanksha has a dedicated team of experienced laboratory technicians and nursing staff

  • The team is passionate about providing a positive and individualized experience for Aakanksha fertility patients along their IVF journey

  • The counselor at Aakanksha understands the concerns and anxiety of the couples undergoing treatment for infertility and thus works closely with the specialist and the staff to ensure the emotional wellbeing of the couples at Aakanksha

Services

Aakanksha team has the expertise in supporting and delivering all fertility related procudures.

We specialize in:

  1. Ovulation induction
  2. IUI (Intra Uterine Insemination)
  3. IVF
  4. ICSI/IMSI
  5. Surgical Sperm Retrieval- PESA/MESA/TESA/TESE
  6. Egg and Embryo Donation
  7. Cryopreservation

Some advanced services such as LAH (Laser Assisted Hatching), Blastocytes Culture, Verification of Embryos, IMSI (Advanced sperm selection technique) and Zona Birefringence (Advanced egg selection technique) are also included
